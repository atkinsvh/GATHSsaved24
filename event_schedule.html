Great American Tiny House Show

Dates:

  • Saturday, June 1, 2024: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Sunday, June 2, 2024: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Location: Fredericksburg Expo & Conference Center, 2371 Carl D. Silver Parkway, Fredericksburg, VA 22401

Event Highlights:

  • Tiny House Tours: Explore various tiny houses on wheels and backyard cottages, including 3D-printed homes by Azure.
  • Tiny House Auction: Bid on the Josephine 26 tiny house from June 1 to June 2. Open to all bidders.
  • DIY Interactive Workshop: Participate in a hands-on workshop to learn how to build a tiny house.
  • Workshops and Presentations: Attend expert-led sessions on sustainable living and tiny house construction.
  • Exhibits: Explore the latest in tiny house technology and sustainable living products.
  • Meet the Builders: Engage with leading tiny house builders and learn about the design and construction process.
